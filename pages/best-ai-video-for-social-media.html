The best tool for social media needs to be fast, mobile-friendly, and loaded with on-trend effects. We ranked all five tools based on social media specific criteria: editing speed, caption quality, trend awareness, platform optimization, and mobile support.

All 5 Tools Ranked for Social Media

🥇 CapCut Pro

CapCut Pro was born for social media. As ByteDance's own editing tool, it has native TikTok integration and deep understanding of what works on social platforms. The auto-caption feature is the best available — it generates accurate, stylized subtitles in seconds with dozens of trendy animation options. The template library is constantly updated with formats that match current social media trends. Cross-platform support means you can capture on mobile, edit anywhere, and publish instantly. At $7.99/month (and a genuinely capable free tier), it's the most efficient social media video tool available.

Try CapCut Pro →

🥈 HeyGen

HeyGen is surprisingly effective for social media marketing. AI avatar videos perform well as ads on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok — the presenter format grabs attention in feeds. The personalization API enables dynamic social media ads at scale. Video translation lets you run the same campaign across global markets. However, HeyGen is better for planned marketing content than reactive social media posts. Use it for ads and campaigns; use CapCut Pro for organic content.

Try HeyGen →

🥉 Descript

Descript can produce social media content, but it's optimized for long-form first. The auto-captions and clip export features work for creating social snippets from longer content (repurposing YouTube videos for Reels, for example). However, the desktop-only limitation and lack of social-native templates make it less efficient for social-first production compared to CapCut Pro.

Try Descript →

#4 Runway

Runway can generate eye-catching visual content for social media — AI-generated clips tend to get high engagement on platforms like TikTok and Instagram due to their novelty. However, the workflow isn't optimized for regular social media production. Best used occasionally for standout creative content rather than daily social media output.

Try Runway →

#5 Synthesia

Synthesia's avatar videos can work for LinkedIn and professional social media content. The corporate presenters suit B2B social media marketing well. However, Synthesia's output style feels too formal for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. It's ranked last for social media because most social platforms favor authentic, casual content that AI avatars struggle to replicate convincingly.

Try Synthesia →
