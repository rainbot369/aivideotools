Overview

Synthesia is the gold standard for enterprise AI video creation. Used by over 50,000 companies including Amazon, Accenture, and BBC, Synthesia has positioned itself as the go-to platform for organizations that need to produce professional training videos, internal communications, and educational content at scale — without the logistics of traditional video production.

The platform's core strength is its unmatched avatar diversity and enterprise-grade reliability. With 230+ pre-built avatars representing different ages, ethnicities, and professional contexts, Synthesia ensures that organizations can create inclusive content that resonates with diverse global workforces. The avatars speak in 160+ languages with remarkably natural intonation and lip-sync, making Synthesia the preferred choice for multinational corporations with training content that needs to reach every region.

What truly sets Synthesia apart from competitors like HeyGen is its enterprise focus. The platform offers SOC 2 compliance, GDPR-ready data processing, single sign-on (SSO), and role-based access controls. For L&D teams and corporate communications departments, these aren't nice-to-haves — they're requirements. Synthesia meets them all while maintaining a surprisingly intuitive interface that non-technical users can master in under an hour.

Key Features

AI Avatars & Custom Presenters

The avatar library is Synthesia's crown jewel. The 230+ stock avatars include full-body and close-up options, each with natural gestures and expressions. For enterprises, custom avatar creation lets you build a digital twin of your CEO, trainer, or spokesperson — perfect for creating consistent internal communications at scale. The Express Avatar feature creates a digital clone from just a few minutes of footage.

Multi-Language Video Production

Synthesia supports 160+ languages and accents, making it the most comprehensive multilingual video platform available. You write your script, select a language, and the avatar delivers it with native-quality pronunciation. For global organizations rolling out compliance training or product updates across dozens of countries, this capability eliminates the need for local voice actors and translation studios.

Video Templates & Collaboration

The template system is designed for enterprise workflows. Teams can create branded templates with locked elements (logos, intros, colors) that individual contributors can populate with their content. The collaboration features include shared workspaces, approval workflows, and version history — everything a large team needs to produce consistent video content without bottlenecks.

AI Script Assistant & Smart Editing

Synthesia includes an AI-powered script assistant that helps you write, refine, and optimize your video scripts. It suggests improvements for clarity, engagement, and pacing. The smart editing feature lets you update specific sections of a video without re-rendering the entire piece — a massive time-saver when you need to update statistics or change a single paragraph in a 20-minute training video.

Who Is Synthesia Best For?

Corporate training teams, L&D departments, enterprise communications, and organizations that need scalable, multilingual video content.

Synthesia is purpose-built for enterprises. If you're producing onboarding videos, compliance training, product walkthroughs, or internal announcements, Synthesia delivers unmatched quality and efficiency. The platform is particularly strong for organizations with 100+ employees who need to maintain consistent messaging across regions and departments. Smaller teams or individual creators may find HeyGen or Descript more flexible and cost-effective.
