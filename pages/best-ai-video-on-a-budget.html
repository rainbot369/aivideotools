All 5 Tools Ranked for Budget

🥇 CapCut Pro

CapCut Pro offers the best budget value in AI video tools — hands down. The free tier is remarkably full-featured, including timeline editing, basic effects, auto-captions, and export capabilities that rival some paid editors. When you're ready to upgrade, Pro costs just $7.99/month on annual billing — less than a single lunch in most cities. For that price, you get all AI features, the full asset library, no watermarks, and cloud storage. There is simply no better value proposition in AI video editing. If budget is your primary constraint, start with CapCut's free tier and upgrade to Pro when you need it.

Try CapCut Pro →

🥈 Runway

Runway's free plan gives you 125 credits to experiment with cutting-edge generative AI video — something that would have cost thousands of dollars in VFX work just a few years ago. The Standard plan at $12/month is also very affordable. The credit-based system means you can control spending tightly. For creators interested in generative AI and visual effects on a budget, Runway delivers remarkable value. Just be aware that credits can run out quickly with heavy use.

Try Runway →

🥉 Descript

Descript's free plan includes 60 minutes of transcription per month and basic editing features — enough to produce several short videos or one longer piece. The Creator plan at $16/month is reasonably priced for the AI features it includes. For budget-conscious YouTubers and podcasters, Descript's free tier is a great starting point. The text-based editing approach actually saves time, which has its own economic value.

Try Descript →

#4 HeyGen

HeyGen's free plan offers 3 videos per month at 720p with a watermark — useful for testing but limited for regular production. The Creator plan at $24/month is mid-range pricing. For budget-conscious creators who specifically need avatar videos, HeyGen's free plan lets you evaluate the platform before committing. But $24/month may be a stretch for hobbyists and very small businesses.

Try HeyGen →

#5 Synthesia

Synthesia's free plan (36 minutes per year) is the most limited of the five tools — it's essentially a demo rather than a usable production tier. The Starter plan at $18/month is reasonable, but the platform is really designed for enterprise budgets. Budget-conscious creators should look at HeyGen or CapCut Pro instead, unless they specifically need Synthesia's enterprise features.

Try Synthesia →
